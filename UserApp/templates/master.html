About Us

  • Mehandi is the art of painting the body in omate designs with heena,assoiciated with transformation and transcendence.
  • We, Pooja Mehandi situated at pune maharashtra, provide Beautiful mehanddi designs as a trendy alternative to tattos.
  • We are proud to be able to evolve from the traditional pattrens of mehandi into more and more intricate and decorative designs in time.
  • The unique designs and intricacy of the mehandi makes it an outstanding work of art.

Why Us

Why Choose Our Service

Skin friendly

We make the heena paste with 100% natural heena powder, lemon juice and essential oils but still deliver great color and shade

Expert Artists

We have artists with expertise in many types of mehandi patterns,most popular ones being the Arebic and maharashtrian designs.

Affordable Prize

Customized price range for all. Easy, affordable and reliable, We offer tremendous value to customers at a reasonable rates.
